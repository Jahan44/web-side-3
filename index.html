LOS ANGELES PROFESSIONAL PHOTOGRAPHER

Hey there, I’m Daihezi Ji. I’m a professional photographer living in Pasadena.

`

Daihezi Ji was born on October 29, 1995. When she moved to America from China when she was 17 it was a huge culture shock. Daihezi took a trip with a camera bought from her friend and fell in love with the medium of photography. The camera became her tool to tell stories and communicate. The experience of living in China and the United States has given her a multicultural perspective. Her visual language is influenced by Chinese aesthetics, drawing on fashion technology, color, traditions, as well as American fashion to create a unique artistic vision.
